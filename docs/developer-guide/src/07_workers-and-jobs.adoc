= Background Workers and Jobs

Capri DAM uses Sidekiq workers for operational pipelines and Active Job for a
small number of Rails-native queue tasks.

== Sidekiq workers

[cols="1,1,3", options="header"]
|===
| Worker | Queue | Responsibility

| `ExtractionWorker`
| default/media
| Existing extractor for technical metadata and downstream asset analysis.

| `AssetProcessorWorker`
| media
| Post-upload processing orchestration for assets.

| `AssetWorkflowTriggerWorker`
| workflows
| Evaluates active workflow triggers after asset processing.

| `WorkflowInitiatorWorker`
| workflows
| Creates `WorkflowInstance` rows and seeds first steps.

| `WorkflowEngineWorker`
| workflows
| Advances automated steps and resolves completion.

| `WorkflowDelayWorker`
| workflows
| Re-enters delayed workflow branches at the requested time.

| `WorkflowSmsWorker`
| notifications/workflows
| SMS notification delivery for workflow nodes.

| `TaskNotificationWorker`
| notifications
| Creates in-app notifications and queues mail for pending tasks.

| `EmailDispatcherWorker`
| mailers
| Renders Liquid templates and performs SMTP delivery with retries.

| `AiBatchJobWorker`
| smartai
| Resolves targets via `Ai::BatchTaskRegistry` and broadcasts a batch dispatch
message to the AI gateway.

| `AiModelHealthCheckWorker`
| smartai
| Publishes provider/model health pings and waits for callback updates.

| `AssetProvenanceWorker`
| smartai
| Publishes single-asset C2PA verification requests.

| `StylePresetSyncWorker`
| smartai
| Broadcasts style-preset definitions to the AI gateway.

| `SmartCollectionRouterWorker`
| smartai
| Recomputes semantic collection membership from `CollectionRule` prompts.

| `DuplicateDetectionWorker`
| default
| Builds/refreshes duplicate groups from checksum data.

| `DuplicateRepositoryScanWorker`
| default
| Long-running repository-wide duplicate scan with status markers in `Setting`.

| `BinPurgeWorker`
| maintenance
| Executes retention-based permanent deletion from the recycle bin.

| `TrashCleanupWorker`
| maintenance
| Secondary cleanup sweep for stale trash data.

| `CdnInvalidationWorker`
| edge
| Sends purge/invalidation requests to the configured CDN adapter.

| `EdgeMetadataSyncWorker`
| edge
| Pushes metadata/signing state to edge caches or provider APIs.

| `FolderMetadataSyncWorker`
| default
| Re-syncs metadata changes across folder trees.

| `IngestionWorker`
| ingestion
| Runs source extraction for an `IngestionBatch`.

| `PreFlightAnalysisWorker`
| ingestion
| Inspects a connector/source and stores readiness/data-health insights.

| `MigrationTransformWorker`
| ingestion
| Applies AI/data normalization during migration review.

| `MigrationCommitWorker`
| ingestion
| Commits reviewed ingestion items into canonical DAM records.

| `MigrationReportWorker`
| ingestion
| Produces migration/review summaries.

| `MetadataExportWorker`
| exports
| Generates CSV metadata exports.

| `MetadataExportCleanupWorker`
| exports
| Removes stale generated export artifacts.

| `MetadataImportWorker`
| imports
| Applies CSV metadata imports to assets.

| `MetadataImportCleanupWorker`
| imports
| Cleans stale source/import files.

| `DataHealthRemediationWorker`
| maintenance
| Executes remediation plans from the Data Health dashboard.
|===

== Active Jobs

[cols="1,3", options="header"]
|===
| Job | Responsibility
| `ApplySchemaToFolderJob` | Applies a `MetadataSchema` to a folder tree and stamps derived schema info into asset properties.
| `PropagateAccessPolicyJob` | Cascades folder-policy changes to descendant folders.
| `PruneAuditLogsJob` | Deletes audit records older than 90 days.
| `Reports::GenerationJob` | Enqueues report generation via `Reports::Orchestrator` on the `reports` queue.
|===

== Common patterns

[source,ruby]
----
AiBatchJobWorker.perform_async(job.id)
WorkflowDelayWorker.perform_in(2.hours, instance.id, next_step_id)
Reports::GenerationJob.perform_later(snapshot.id)
----

IMPORTANT: Gateway-driven callbacks are authenticated separately from user
sessions. See controllers such as `AgentWorkflowsController`,
`AiBatchJobsController`, and `AiModelConfigsController` for the shared-secret
pattern.
