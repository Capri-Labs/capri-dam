== Getting Started

You've now seen what Capri DAM can do. Here's how to get your first asset uploaded,
organized, and shared in the next five minutes.

=== 1. Feature Overview

**What it does:** This section walks you through your very first login, upload, and
share — the fastest path to your first "wow" moment.

**The business value:** You'll see AI tagging, semantic search, and instant sharing
working on your own content before you finish your first cup of coffee.

=== 2. How It Works

**The trigger:** Your very first sign-in to Capri DAM.

**What happens:** Once you're in, uploading a file automatically kicks off the
same AI enrichment and duplicate-detection pipeline described in
<<module-cg-ai-smart-uploads>> and <<module-cg-duplicate-manager>> — there's no
separate "turn on AI" step.

**The output:** A fully tagged, searchable, shareable asset — ready for your team.

=== 3. Step-by-Step Instructions

. **Log in** using the credentials your administrator provided (or your
  organisation's SSO button, if enabled — see <<module-cg-access-control>>).
. From the **Dashboard** (see <<module-cg-dashboard>>), click **Upload Assets**.
. Drag a file onto the upload zone, or click to browse.
. Watch the progress bar reach 100%, then watch for the green **Enriched** badge —
  that's the AI finishing its analysis.
. Click into the asset to review its **auto-generated tags** and correct any that
  don't fit.
. Try the **search bar** with a plain-English description of the image (e.g. "people
  eating in a park") to see semantic search in action.
. Click **Share** to generate a link for a colleague — no account required on their
  end.

=== 4. Options & Configuration

* Revisit your **Language** preference at any time (see <<module-cg-localization>>).
* Ask an admin about **Workflow Automation** (<<module-cg-workflow>>) if your team
  needs an approval step before assets go live.
* Explore **Collections** (<<module-cg-collections>>) once you have a handful of
  assets, to build your first shareable campaign kit.

=== 5. Access & Permissions

**Roles required:** Every invited user can log in, upload, and share immediately —
no special onboarding step is gated behind an admin.

**Restrictions:** What you can see and do beyond your own uploads depends on your
group's folder permissions — see <<module-cg-access-control>> if something looks
missing.

=== 6. System Limits & Technical Requirements

* Default maximum upload size is 2GB per file (your admin may adjust this).
* AI Vision tagging applies to image formats; see <<module-cg-ai-smart-uploads>> for
  the full supported-format list.

=== 7. Troubleshooting & FAQs

**I don't see an Upload button. What's wrong?**
You may not have Contributor access on your current folder — ask your admin to check
your group's permissions (<<module-cg-access-control>>).

**Where can I get help beyond this guide?**
See the companion link:../api-troubleshooting.md[API & Integration Troubleshooting
Guide], or the deeper link:../product-info/index.adoc[technical Product Information
guide] if you or your team need implementation-level detail.

Welcome aboard — happy uploading!
