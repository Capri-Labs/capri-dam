=== Powerful Search & Discovery

==== 1. Feature Overview

**What it does:** One global search bar gives you three ways to find what you need —
keyword search, visual similarity matching, and natural-language AI chat.

**The business value:** However you think about the asset you need, there's a fast way
to find it, from any screen in the app.

image::search-and-discovery.png[Powerful Search & Discovery, title="Placeholder — global search with keyword, visual, and AI modes (screenshot coming soon)"]

==== 2. How It Works

**The trigger:** Click the search bar (present on every page) or press
kbd:[Cmd+K] / kbd:[Ctrl+K].

**What the system does:** Depending on the selected mode, your query is matched
against full-text metadata (Keyword mode), visual/semantic similarity (Visual Match
mode), or interpreted by the AI Copilot into structured filters (Ask AI Agent mode).

**The output:** Matching results render in a responsive grid on the `/search` page,
with the query and mode preserved in the URL so results are shareable and
bookmarkable.

==== 3. Step-by-Step Instructions

. Click the **search bar** or press kbd:[Cmd+K].
. Choose a mode from the dropdown: **Keyword**, **Visual Match**, or **Ask AI Agent**.
. Type your query — suggestions appear as you type.
. Press kbd:[Enter] or click **View all results** to go to the full results page.
. Use the **facet filters** (content type, date, tags, folder) to narrow further.
. Optionally toggle **Include Recycle Bin** to also search soft-deleted assets.

==== 4. Options & Configuration

* **Results per page** — choose 25, 50, or 100 results per page (default 25).
* **Include Recycle Bin** — off by default; enable to include soft-deleted assets,
  clearly flagged with a "Bin" badge.
* **Semantic cluster map** — an alternative, visual way to explore related assets
  instead of a flat results list.

==== 5. Access & Permissions

**Roles required:** Available to all signed-in users. Results are automatically
scoped to folders you have Read access to — search never reveals assets you don't
already have permission to view.

==== 6. System Limits & Technical Requirements

* **Semantic search quality** depends on your organisation's configured AI embedding
  provider; there is no automatic fallback if that provider is unreachable.
* **Semantic cluster map** is best suited to small-to-medium result sets — very large
  clusters (1,000+ nodes) are not yet paginated.

==== 7. Troubleshooting & FAQs

**Why did my search return no results?**
Check that your query isn't overly specific — shorter, more generic terms tend to work
best. Also confirm the content-type mode (Images vs. Files) matches what you're
looking for.

**Can I search inside the Recycle Bin?**
Yes — enable **Include Recycle Bin** in the search bar; matching bin items are clearly
flagged so they're never confused with active assets.
