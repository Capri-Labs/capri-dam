=== What Makes Capri DAM Different

==== Overview

**What it does:** Capri DAM is an intelligent partner for your creative and marketing
teams — it automatically organises, de-duplicates, and delivers your assets, instead
of leaving that work to you.

**The business value:** Less time spent on file admin means more time spent on the
work that actually grows your brand.

Three ideas sit behind everything in this guide:

[.feature-highlight]
=====

* **Zero manual tagging** — AI does the tedious organisational work, so assets are
  searchable the moment they land.
* **Zero clutter** — duplicates, retouches, and re-exports are recognised and grouped
  automatically, keeping your library clean without anyone lifting a finger.
* **Zero wait** — assets are delivered instantly, anywhere in the world, at exactly the
  size and format each screen needs.
=====

==== How to read each module

Every module chapter in this guide follows the same seven-part structure:

[.feature-highlight]
=====

. **Feature Overview** — the "what" and "why," in two sentences.
. **How It Works** — the trigger, what the system does, and where the result goes.
. **Step-by-Step Instructions** — the exact clicks, in order.
. **Options & Configuration** — the controls you have over the feature.
. **Access & Permissions** — who can see or use it.
. **System Limits & Technical Requirements** — the boundaries, stated plainly.
. **Troubleshooting & FAQs** — what to expect when something doesn't go as planned.
=====
