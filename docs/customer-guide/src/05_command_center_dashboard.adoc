=== Command Center Dashboard

==== 1. Feature Overview

**What it does:** A single home screen summarises your entire library the moment you
sign in — totals, growth trends, content mix, and pending approvals.

**The business value:** You know the health of your library at a glance, without
opening a single folder.

image::command-center-dashboard.png[Command Center Dashboard, title="Placeholder — the Dashboard home screen (screenshot coming soon)"]

==== 2. How It Works

**The trigger:** The Dashboard loads automatically as your post-login landing page.

**What the system does:** It queries your library's current statistics (asset counts,
storage used, content-type mix) and recent workflow activity, and renders them as stat
cards and charts.

**The output:** A "Last updated" timestamp is shown alongside a manual **Refresh**
control, so you always know how current the numbers are.

==== 3. Step-by-Step Instructions

. Sign in — the Dashboard is shown automatically.
. Review the **stat cards** for a quick library snapshot.
. Check the **Asset Growth** chart for the trailing six months.
. Check the **Workflow Summary** for pending approvals needing attention.
. Click **Refresh** at any time to pull the latest numbers.

==== 4. Options & Configuration

* The Dashboard adapts its card set and insights based on your role and permissions —
  there are currently no user-configurable widgets to add, remove, or rearrange.

==== 5. Access & Permissions

**Roles required:** Available to every signed-in user; the specific cards and insights
shown adapt to what that user's role and permissions allow them to see.

==== 6. System Limits & Technical Requirements

* The Asset Growth chart currently buckets by calendar month only — there is no custom
  date-range picker yet.
* "AI Insights" on the Dashboard is a rule-based system-health summary, not a
  generative report — the more conversational AI Copilot lives in its own area (see
  the Product Info guide's AI Intelligence Suite chapter).

==== 7. Troubleshooting & FAQs

**My stat cards look out of date. What do I do?**
Click **Refresh** — the Dashboard shows a "Last updated" timestamp rather than
auto-refreshing continuously, to avoid unnecessary load.

**Can I customise which cards I see?**
Not today — the card set is determined by your role, not individually configurable.
