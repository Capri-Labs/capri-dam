=== Localization & Multi-Language Support

==== 1. Feature Overview

**What it does:** The entire interface — menus, buttons, messages, and emails — is
available in 10 languages, switchable per user, instantly.

**The business value:** Global teams work in the language they're most comfortable
in, with no separate regional deployments required.

image::localization-i18n.png[Localization & Multi-Language Support, title="Placeholder — the language switcher (screenshot coming soon)"]

==== 2. How It Works

**The trigger:** Choosing a language from your Profile settings (or your browser's
detected locale on first login).

**What the system does:** The interface immediately re-renders every label, button,
and message from the selected language's translation bundle — no reload or re-login
required.

**The output:** Your language preference is remembered for future sessions and
carries through to system emails you receive.

==== 3. Step-by-Step Instructions

. Open your **Profile** menu (top-right avatar).
. Click **Preferences**.
. Choose your language from the **Language** dropdown.
. The interface updates immediately — no need to refresh the page.

==== 4. Options & Configuration

* **Per-user preference** — each person on your team can pick their own language
  independently.
* **Supported languages** — English, German, Spanish, French, Arabic, Japanese,
  Korean, Dutch, Portuguese, and Chinese.
* Right-to-left layout is applied automatically when Arabic is selected.

==== 5. Access & Permissions

**Roles required:** Any signed-in user can change their own language preference —
no special role is needed.

**Restrictions:** Language preference is personal — it cannot be set organisation-wide
to force a single language on all users (each user chooses their own).

==== 6. System Limits & Technical Requirements

* All 10 supported locales are maintained with **full translation parity** — the
  product does not ship a language with partial coverage.
* Locale switching affects the interface and system emails; it does not translate the
  content of your actual assets (e.g. filenames, metadata values you've entered).

==== 7. Troubleshooting & FAQs

**I changed my language, but some text is still in English. Is that a bug?**
Please report it — the product is maintained with full translation parity across all
10 languages, so any leftover English text is treated as a bug, not an expected gap.

**Can I set a default language for my whole organisation?**
Not currently — language is a per-user preference; each teammate selects their own.
