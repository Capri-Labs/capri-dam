[[section-welcome]]
== Welcome to Zero-Noise Asset Management

Every day, creative teams lose hours to the same frustrating ritual: hunting for the
"final_final_v3" file, re-uploading an image someone already uploaded last month, or
waiting on a designer to email over a logo buried three folders deep in a shared drive.

**Capri DAM** was built to make that ritual disappear. We call our design philosophy
**"Zero-Noise."** It means your asset library should be instantly available, visually
uncluttered, and quietly intelligent — doing the tedious work of tagging, organising,
and delivering your content so your team can spend its time on the work that actually
matters: the creative itself.

This guide is a module-by-module tour of Capri DAM for customers, evaluators, and new
team members. Every module chapter follows the same structure so you can jump straight
to what you need:

[.feature-highlight]
====
**How to read each module**

* **The Feature** — what it does, in plain language.
* **Screenshot** — a real capture of the running application (placeholders are used
  until a module's screenshot has been captured — see <<section-screenshots>>).
* **The Benefit** — why it matters to your day-to-day work.
* **Highlights** — the handful of things that set this capability apart.
====
